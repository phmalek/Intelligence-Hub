## Objective

Create a client-facing update deck that explains:
- where UTM adoption follow-ups currently stand
- how local markets have responded
- where internal coordination is required
- what happens next

The audience is a senior client stakeholder. The deck must be easy to follow, visually clean, and obviously grounded in the actual follow-up tracker.

## Source

- `UTM-followup-wrapup.xlsx`

## Design Direction

- premium, minimal, structured
- dark graphite base with restrained white/grey typography
- one accent blue reserved for internal coordination
- plain, decisive language
- no cluttered tables unless they genuinely help the story

## Story

1. UTM adoption follow-up is active across markets
2. Most markets are engaged, but they are not all at the same stage
3. The main blockers are repeatable and understandable
4. Some items now require internal coordination, not just market follow-up
5. The next step is a focused closure plan by market type

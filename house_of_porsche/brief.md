2. CREATIVE OVERVIEW: HAUS OF PORSCHE


The Story

For the first time in its 80-year history, Porsche grants unprecedented access into its inner world. "Porsche: In The Making" is a high-stakes, character-driven documentary series that follows Porsche as it builds the cars and company that will define the next century. The series moves between two timelines:

• Present Day: Real-time challenges as Porsche navigates the greatest disruption the automotive industry has faced—electric vehicles, new luxury definitions, and market transformation

• Historical: Pivotal moments from Porsche's past that explain why today's challenges matter and how the brand has reinvented itself before
